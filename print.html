In the print project we had to create a custom bookmark for Marticville Middle School. The bookmark had to have an inspiring message for the students at Marticville.

First we had to sketch five tumbnail sketches, then select our best sketch and make a rough layout in InDesign.

After the rough layout we had to make a final result of our bookmark. We ran these final bookmarks through a press which finalized this project.
